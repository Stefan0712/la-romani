About us

With hearthwarming we are waiting for you at our store "La Români", the place where you can find authentic products from Romania, such as food, drinks and snacks. We are a small local business situated in Cochem and we are proud to bring Romanian snacks and drinks closer to you. No matter if you are a romanian who misses home or a tourist wanting to try something new, our store is the right place for you.

The store "La Români" has a diverse collection of products, such as:

  • Meats
  • Drinks
  • Sweets
  • Snacks
  • Spices
  • Essentials
